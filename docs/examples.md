# Example prompts

A growing collection of prompts that work well. Have a good one? Add it.

## Make YouTube Shorts from a long video

> Make clips for YouTube Shorts from this video, with burned-in subtitles:
> https://youtu.be/r1Kh5WssSPg

> Find the three strongest moments in this talk and turn each into a vertical
> clip under 60 seconds, captioned, ready for Shorts and Reels:
> recordings/talk.mp4

## Edit a podcast recording into an episode

> Edit this podcast recording into a publishable episode: cut the false starts,
> retakes, and long silences, keep the conversation intact, and master the
> audio. The raw file is recordings/episode-12.mp4.

## Remove dead time from a screen recording

> Tighten this screen recording into a demo video: remove the dead time where
> nothing happens on screen and the moments where I lose my train of thought.
> demos/onboarding-walkthrough.mov

## Sync and cut a multi-camera interview

> I recorded this interview with two cameras and a separate audio recorder.
> Sync them, use the audio recorder's track, and cut between cameras so the
> person speaking is on screen. Files are in raw/interview/.

## Transcribe a video and burn in subtitles

> Transcribe this video and give me an SRT file, plus a copy of the video with
> the subtitles burned in: lecture.mp4

## Denoise and master audio for a podcast

> Extract the audio from this video, denoise it, and master it for podcast
> distribution: recordings/remote-guest.mp4

## Inspect a folder of unknown media files

> I found this folder of media files from an old shoot and have no idea what's
> in them. Inspect everything in footage/2023-shoot/ and give me a summary of
> each file: duration, streams, and what it appears to contain.
